Frequently Asked Questions

What is included in a permit-ready design package?

Typically architectural floor plans, elevations, sections, site layout, and documentation aligned with local authority requirements (e.g. Kubaka in Rwanda). Scope varies by project type.

How long does a residential design take?

Standard family homes often take 2–4 weeks after site details are confirmed. Larger or custom projects may take longer — we provide a timeline at consultation.

Can I use the cost estimator for final budgeting?

The estimator gives a maximum indicative construction budget in RWF based on dimensions you enter. For binding quotes, contact us with your full brief.

Do you work outside Rwanda?

Yes. We serve clients across Africa with offices in Kigali, Rwanda and Accra, Ghana. Remote collaboration and site visits can be arranged.

How do I purchase or license a design from your catalog?

Browse trending designs, save favorites with + Save, then reach out via WhatsApp or the contact form. See our shopping guide (PDF) for the full process.
